LOCATION

BY TRAIN:

Take a train to Würzburg central station (“Hauptbahnhof”).

Our office is about 100 m on foot from the railway station.

BY CAR:

Drive to the center (“Zentrum”) of Würzburg. On the outskirts you could follow the signs “Hauptbahnhof” or “Congress-Zentrum”. When you arrive in the center, drive to the “Hauptbahnhof” and use the car park. From there, walk 100m to our office.

BY PLANE:

Fly to Frankfurt International Airport (FRA). Continue by train for about 1h eastbound to Würzburg Hauptbahnhof. Walk 100m to our office.
