PRACTICE AREAS

Patents

TECHNICS UND PATENT „The right wording of a problem is already half the solution!”, Albert Einstein probably said. And if the complete solution is a technically brilliant idea, it again has to be described with the „right” wording to make it understandable for others. Only with such a wording can it be protected for the inventor. LOCAL AND GLOBAL PROTECTION National patents have existed for a long time. Today, the European Patent Convention and the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) have made it possible to protect inventions with one single application over entire Europe, and in all major industrialized countries of the world. For a globally valid application of a patent, you can rely on standard regulations. European patents and PCT applications thus make a significant contribution to the global competitiveness of a patent holder.

Trademarks

We offer our clients a comprehensive consultation and representation services in the field of trademarks in all phases of the proceedings from the registration, opposition, cancellation and infringement. We are your legal representative in all affairs concerning the DPMA in Munich, Germany (German Patent and Trade Mark Office), the EUIPO in Alicante, Spain (European Union Intellectual Property Office) and the WIPO in Geneva (World Intellectual Property Organization) With the assistance of other patent- and trademark attorney offices in countries outside of Germany, we can organize the registration of your trade marks and the protection of your Intellectual Property rights outside of Germany. Our office represents clients and their trademarks in the fields of consumer products, agriculture, fashion industry, medical technology, electronics, machine-building, biology and others.
