PRACTICE AREAS

Patents

„The right wording of a problem is already half the solution!”, Albert Einstein probably said. Similarly, when the solution itself is a technically ingenious idea, it must be conveyed using the appropriate terminology to ensure its clarity for others. It's only through such precise wording that protection for the inventor's creation can be established.

LOCAL AND GLOBAL PROTECTION – National patents have a longstanding history. Presently, thanks to the European Patent Convention and the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT), safeguarding inventions across all of Europe and major industrialized nations worldwide can be achieved through a single application. To ensure a universally applicable patent, standardized regulations come into play. European patents and PCT applications, therefore, substantially enhance the worldwide competitiveness of a patent holder.

Designs

We assist with applications to register your designs

We represent you in infringement and cancellation proceedings, which we conduct on your behalf before the German Patent and Trade Mark Office (DPMA), the European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO), and the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO).

We cooperate with other Intellectual Property offices to register design patents in countries outside of Germany.

Other IP related law

Trademarks

We offer our clients a comprehensive consultation and representation services in the field of trademarks in all phases of the proceedings from the registration, opposition, cancellation and infringement.

We are your legal representative in all affairs concerning the DPMA in Munich, Germany (German Patent and Trade Mark Office), the EUIPO in Alicante, Spain (European Union Intellectual Property Office) and the WIPO in Geneva (World Intellectual Property Organization)

With the assistance of other patent- and trademark attorney offices in countries outside of Germany, we can organize the registration of your trademarks and the protection of your industrial property rights outside of Germany.

Our office represents clients and their trademarks in the fields of consumer products, agriculture, fashion industry, medical technology, electronics, machine-building, biology and others.
